## 0.1.0

* Initial public API: `LoadingStyle` with five presets and `AnkaSuperLoading` widget.
* Loaders: pulse orbit, ring stroke, shimmer bar, segment spinner, wave dots.
* Respects `MediaQuery.disableAnimations` with static fallback frames.
* Example gallery app with per-style tuning (size, duration, colour) and light/dark/system theme.
